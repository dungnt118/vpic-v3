FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Q.01

Does VPIC have a minimum order quantity?

First-time order (market trial) from 5 tons per SKU. Annual orders have no hard minimum — VPIC schedules per production plan.

Q.02

Do customers keep formulations if switching manufacturer?

Fully custom formulations developed for a customer belong to that customer — VPIC commits this in NDA. Standard base formulations remain VPIC's property.

Q.03

Does VPIC support label design?

VPIC produces and packages per customer-supplied design files. If design assistance is needed, we refer experienced partners.

Q.04

Do products have certifications and declarations?

VPIC supports customers in filing quality declarations with the relevant authority. Cost and time per local regulations.

Q.05

Payment terms?

First order: 50% deposit + 50% before delivery. Long-term partners may negotiate 15-30 day credit after framework contract.

Q.06

Does VPIC accept export-market manufacturing?

Yes — VPIC has experience exporting to several ASEAN markets. Specific requirements per destination country to be discussed.
