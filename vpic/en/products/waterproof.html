Contract manufacturing of waterproof coatings.

Our waterproof range covers both waterborne and solvent-based systems for the full spectrum of civil and industrial applications — from external walls and rooftops to potable water tanks.

VPIC offers everything from standard base formulations (ready to brand and bottle) to fully custom formulations developed to your brief — including technical specifications, binder system, color system, and any special test requirements.

A — Product lines

The waterproof coatings systems we manufacture.

01

Waterborne Acrylic

Waterborne waterproof coating with 200-400% elongation, strong adhesion to concrete, mortar, and brick. Suitable for external walls and rooftops exposed to sun and rain.

200-400% elongationFast dryUV resistant
02

Polyurethane (PU) 1K/2K

Premium system for flat roofs, water tanks, high-load structures. Mechanical strength and chemical resistance significantly exceed Acrylic.

PU 1KPU 2KAliphatic PU
03

Two-component cement-based

Cement + polymer hybrid, penetrates deeply into concrete. Used for basements, submerged tanks, swimming pools — high water pressure positions.

Submerged concreteSwimming poolsBasements
04

Modified bitumen

Modified bitumen-based for metal roofs, gutters, exposed slabs. High elongation, tolerates thermal cycling and structural movement.

Metal roofsGuttersBridge decks

C — Applications

Common applications.

01

External walls

Facade walls, multi-story residential building sides exposed to weather.

02

Rooftops / terraces

Flat roofs and terraces with or without light foot traffic.

03

Water tanks

Potable water tanks, underground tanks, fish ponds — requiring food-grade contact.

04

Basements / elevator pits

Negative water pressure positions — use cement-based two-component system.

05

Expansion joints

Building expansion joints and panel seams — use elastic PU.

06

Bathroom floors

Waterproof layer under tiles, preventing seepage to floors below.

07

Gutters / metal roofs

Use modified bitumen on metal surfaces subject to thermal expansion.

08

Foundations / underground walls

Structural concrete protection for foundations and retaining walls.
