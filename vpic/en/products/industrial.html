Contract manufacturing of industrial coatings.

Protective coatings designed for steel structures, pipelines, industrial equipment, and infrastructure. Meeting international standards including ISO 12944, NORSOK, and NACE.

VPIC offers everything from standard base formulations (ready to brand and bottle) to fully custom formulations developed to your brief — including technical specifications, binder system, color system, and any special test requirements.

Common applications.

01

Factories / steel structures

Steel frames, roof trusses, columns of industrial workshops.

02

Bridges / pipelines

Steel bridges, oil and gas transmission lines, industrial water pipes.

Ports / oil & gas
