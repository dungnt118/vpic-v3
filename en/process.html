Home / Process

Six steps from brief to first batch.

VPIC runs a clear process with timeline commitments at every step. Customers always know exactly where they stand in the pipeline — no black box, no surprises.

01

Receive brief

Customer submits brief including: product description, target volume, required specs, reference samples (if any), packaging.

02

R&D proposal

Engineering team analyzes brief, proposes candidate formulations, indicative cost, expected sample timeline.

03

Lab sample

Mix lab samples at 1-5kg scale. Check appearance, wet and dry properties. Send sample to customer for evaluation.

04

Approval & contract

Final sample approved in writing. Sign framework contract or specific order.

05

Bulk production

Production planning, material procurement, batch production. Per-batch QC, customer packaging.

06

Delivery & support

Delivery on agreed schedule. Each batch ships with official COA. Lifetime technical support.

B — What to prepare

What customers should bring.

The more complete your initial information, the faster the process. Minimum information needed for VPIC to start:

01

Product description

Working product name, positioning (economy / mid / premium), main selling points.

02

Required specifications

Hard requirements (elongation, coverage, UV resistance, etc.). If not yet defined, VPIC consults.

03

Reference samples

Competitor samples or current products. Helps R&D position quickly.

04

Expected volume

First-time order and annual order — affects pricing and production scheduling.

05

Packaging & branding

Pack size (1L / 5L / 18L / drums), customer's brand label design files.

06

Time to market

Launch deadline — VPIC works back from this to commit timeline.
