Home / About VPIC

The contract paint manufacturer behind many Vietnam brands.

VPIC was created with a single mission: provide high-quality manufacturing capacity for protective coatings to Vietnamese paint brands that prefer to focus their resources on market, distribution, and brand-building — rather than investing in a factory.

01 — Our story

A different kind of partnership.

Vietnam's paint market has hundreds of brands — but most do not own a factory. They are strong in brand-building, distribution, and sales — but investing in a proper protective coating factory requires tens to hundreds of billions of VND and deep technical expertise.

VPIC exists to solve that problem. We do not sell paint under our own brand — we are the factory behind. Customers keep their brand, their channels, their end-customers; VPIC takes full responsibility for formulation, production, and quality control.

We focus on protective coatings — waterproof, industrial, epoxy, specialty — because this is where the technical barriers are high, the products are hard to get right, and demand is growing alongside Vietnam's civil and industrial construction wave.

VPIC's leadership team has 15+ years in the paint industry, having led production for major Vietnamese paint brands. We speak both the technical language of the lab and the commercial pressure of a brand CEO.

03 — Why choose VPIC

Six reasons Vietnam brands choose VPIC.

01

Protective coating focus

No distractions — VPIC only does protective and functional coatings. Every investment in equipment, lab, and people targets this niche.

02

No brand competition

VPIC does not sell paint under our own brand. Customers face no channel conflict and no risk of formulation leaking to competitors.

03

Transparent pricing

Clear cost structure by materials, labor, and overhead. When raw material prices move, customers know exactly why and by how much.

04

Post-sale technical support

When customers face complaints from the market, VPIC sends engineers to the site to inspect, assess, and resolve.

05

Flexible volume

Orders from a few tons (trial projects) up to thousands of tons per year. No rigid MOQ requirements.

06

Formulation confidentiality

Custom formulations for each customer are stored independently under NDA. Never shared, never reused for other customers.
