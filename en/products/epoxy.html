VPIC / EPOXY & FLOOR
EPX

Contract manufacturing of epoxy and floor coatings.

Epoxy floor systems are the standard for factories, hospitals, cold storage, parking, and any area demanding hygiene, chemical resistance, or wear durability. VPIC manufactures both roller and self-leveling systems.

VPIC offers everything from standard base formulations (ready to brand and bottle) to fully custom formulations developed to your brief — including technical specifications, binder system, color system, and any special test requirements.
